1. Preventive Maintenance Schedule

Following the preventive maintenance schedule ensures optimal performance and extends equipment life.

Frequency Task Duration
Daily Visual inspection, clean work area 10 minutes
Weekly Check cable conditions, clean sensors 20 minutes
Monthly Lubrication, check bolt torque 45 minutes
Quarterly Detailed inspection, calibration check 2 hours
Annually Complete system overhaul, replace wear items 4-6 hours

6. Troubleshooting Common Issues

Common issues and their solutions:

Robot Won't Start

  • Check power supply and circuit breakers
  • Verify emergency stop is not engaged
  • Check controller display for error codes
  • Inspect all safety interlocks

Position Errors

  • Perform home position calibration
  • Check encoder batteries
  • Verify mechanical backlash is within spec
  • Inspect for loose mounting bolts

Jerky Motion

  • Check lubrication levels
  • Inspect cables for damage
  • Verify payload is within specifications
  • Review acceleration/deceleration settings
