Installation Instructions

Step 1: Site Preparation

Ensure mounting surface can support 1500kg static load. Minimum concrete thickness: 200mm. Ambient temperature range: 5°C to 45°C.

Step 2: Base Installation

  • Use provided M16 anchor bolts
  • Torque to 147 Nm ±10%
  • Ensure base is level within 0.1°

Step 3: Electrical Connections

All electrical connections must be performed by certified electricians.

  • Connect 3-phase power (380-480VAC)
  • Install emergency stop circuit
  • Connect safety barrier interlocks
  • Verify ground connection (≤100Ω)

Pre-Operation Safety Checklist

  • Verify emergency stop functionality
  • Check safety barrier operation
  • Confirm work envelope clearance (minimum 2m safety zone)
  • Test teach pendant controls
  • Verify all axis movements at 10% speed

Maintenance Schedule

Daily Checks

  • Visual inspection of cables
  • Emergency stop verification
  • Check for unusual noise/vibration

Monthly Maintenance

  • Lubricate all axis points
  • Check belt tension
  • Verify encoder readings
  • Clean ventilation filters
