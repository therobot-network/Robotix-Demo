Robotix Warranty and Return Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Limited Warranty Coverage

Robotix warrants its products to be free from defects in materials and workmanship under normal use and maintenance. This warranty extends only to the original purchaser and is non-transferable.

Warranty Terms by Product Category

  • Robot Chassis/Frames: Lifetime limited warranty on structural components
  • Electronic Components: 2-year limited warranty on circuit boards, motors, and control systems
  • Software: 1-year limited warranty covering major functionality and security updates
  • Accessories: 1-year limited warranty on peripheral devices and attachments

Wear items explicitly excluded: cables, sensors subject to mechanical wear, consumables, batteries.

What is NOT Covered

  • Normal wear and tear from regular operation
  • Damage resulting from improper installation, misuse, or operation outside specifications
  • Lack of recommended maintenance as outlined in product documentation
  • Unauthorized modifications or third-party component integration
  • Environmental damage (water, lightning, power surges)
  • Commercial use exceeding duty cycle specifications
  • Theft, vandalism, or accidental damage
  • Consequential damages including lost production time or data

Warranty Claims Process

  1. Initial Contact: Email warranty@robotix.com or call 1-800-555-ROBO
  2. Required Documentation:
    • Proof of purchase
    • Product serial number
    • Detailed description of issue
    • Photos/videos of problem
  3. Evaluation Period: 3-5 business days
  4. Resolution: Repair, replacement, or credit as determined by technical team

Equipment Replacement Program

For catastrophic chassis damage within first year of ownership:

  • 50% discount on replacement unit
  • One-time offer per customer
  • $199 processing fee applies
  • Original equipment must be returned

30-Day Return Policy

Return Eligibility Requirements:

  • Item must be unused and in original condition
  • All original packaging and accessories included
  • Return initiated within 30 days of delivery
  • Valid proof of purchase required

Non-Returnable Items:

  • Used or operated equipment
  • Custom-configured systems
  • Clearance items
  • Activated software licenses

Return Process

  1. Initiate return through online portal or customer service
  2. Package item securely in original packaging
  3. Ship using provided return label
  4. Allow 5-7 business days for refund processing

Refund Terms

  • Full refund to original payment method
  • Original shipping costs non-refundable
  • 15% restocking fee for opened industrial equipment
  • Damaged returns subject to evaluation

International Returns

International customers are responsible for:

  • Return shipping costs
  • Customs duties and fees
  • Proper export documentation

Limitation of Liability

Robotix's liability is limited to repair, replacement, or refund of the original purchase price. We are not liable for consequential damages including but not limited to lost production time, data loss, or business interruption.
