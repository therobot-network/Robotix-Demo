Robotix Employee Handbook

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Welcome to Robotix

Welcome to Robotix - Automate Your Future! Since 1998, we've been at the forefront of robotics innovation, creating automated solutions that transform industries. This handbook outlines our policies and your benefits as a valued team member.

Our Mission & Values

To revolutionize automation through innovative robotics solutions while maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, and sustainability.

Core Values:
  • Innovation Excellence
  • Quality Without Compromise
  • Sustainable Manufacturing
  • Safety First
  • Continuous Learning

Employment Basics

Work Schedule

Standard hours are 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM Pacific Time, Monday through Friday. Manufacturing shifts operate in three rotations: 6:00 AM - 2:30 PM, 2:30 PM - 11:00 PM, and 11:00 PM - 6:30 AM.

Dress Code

Office staff: Business casual. Manufacturing floor: Company-provided safety gear including steel-toed boots, safety glasses, and ESD-protective clothing must be worn at all times.

Manufacturing Safety Requirements

  • Safety certification required for all manufacturing floor personnel
  • Monthly safety training updates
  • Proper PPE must be worn in designated areas
  • Robot operation certification required for testing areas

Compensation & Benefits

Benefits Overview

  • Medical, dental, and vision insurance (coverage begins first of month after hire date)
  • 401(k) with 4% company match
  • Life insurance (2x annual salary)
  • Short and long-term disability
  • Employee Stock Purchase Program (15% discount)
  • 40% employee discount on Robotix products

Time Off

PTO:15 days annually
Sick Leave:10 days annually
Holidays:8 paid holidays

Professional Development

Annual development budget of $1,000 for approved courses, certifications, or conferences. Additional robotics-specific certifications may be fully covered based on role requirements.

Required Certifications

  • RIA Robot Safety Certification (manufacturing roles)
  • ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management training
  • Cybersecurity awareness training

Remote Work Policy

Remote work is available for eligible office-based positions. Manufacturing, quality control, and lab positions require on-site presence. Hybrid schedules available for eligible roles with minimum 2 days on-site.

Safety Protocols

Manufacturing Floor Safety

  • Never enter robot operating zones without proper lockout/tagout procedures
  • Maintain minimum 2-meter distance from active robots
  • Emergency stop procedures must be memorized and tested quarterly
  • Report all incidents, regardless of severity

Laboratory Safety

  • Eye wash stations and chemical showers located at marked positions
  • Chemical safety protocols must be followed for all testing procedures
  • No food or drinks in lab areas

Employee Conduct

All employees must maintain professional conduct, adhere to safety protocols, and protect confidential information. Zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination.

Performance Reviews

Bi-annual reviews conducted in June and December. Technical competency assessments required annually for engineering and manufacturing roles.
