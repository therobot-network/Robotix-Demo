SmartCart AMR-1000 Technical Specification

Product Overview

The SmartCart AMR-1000 is an advanced autonomous mobile robot designed for intelligent material handling and transportation in manufacturing, warehousing, and logistics environments. Featuring our proprietary NaviCore™ navigation system and adaptive payload management, the AMR-1000 delivers exceptional flexibility and reliability for modern industrial applications.

Key Applications

  • Automated material transport in manufacturing facilities
  • Warehouse order fulfillment
  • Cross-docking operations
  • Just-in-time delivery systems
  • Production line supply management

Drive System

  • Dual 48V brushless DC motors
