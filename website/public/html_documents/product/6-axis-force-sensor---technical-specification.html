Product Overview

The RBX-FCS-2024 6-Axis Force/Torque Sensor is a high-precision measurement device designed for advanced robotic applications requiring detailed force and torque feedback. This sensor provides real-time measurement of forces and torques in all six degrees of freedom (Fx, Fy, Fz, Tx, Ty, Tz).

Key Applications

  • Robotic assembly operations
  • Quality control and testing
  • Research and development
  • Automated material handling
  • Surface finishing and polishing
