1. Product Overview

The CoBot Dual-Arm System is an advanced collaborative robot designed for complex assembly, material handling, and precision manufacturing applications. Featuring dual 7-axis arms with integrated force sensing and advanced vision systems, the CoBot delivers unprecedented flexibility and safety for human-robot collaboration.

1.1 Target Applications

  • Automotive component assembly
  • Electronics manufacturing
  • Medical device assembly
  • Laboratory automation
  • Small parts handling and testing

2. Technical Specifications

2.1 Mechanical Specifications

Frame Construction Aircraft-grade aluminum alloy with carbon fiber components
Weight 120 kg (base unit)
Reach 850mm per arm
Payload 8 kg per arm
Degrees of Freedom 7 per arm (14 total)

2.2 Control System

  • Controller: RBX-CTRL-2000 Industrial PC
  • Operating System: RobotixOS 4.2 (Linux-based)
  • Programming Interface: RobotixStudio 7.0
  • Communication: EtherCAT, Profinet, Ethernet/IP, USB 3.0

2.3 Sensor Systems

  • Integrated force/torque sensors (0.1N resolution)
  • Dual 3D vision cameras (2MP, 60fps)
  • Joint position encoders (0.01° resolution)
  • Proximity sensors for collision detection

3. Performance Metrics

Maximum Speed 2.0 m/s (tool center point)
Repeatability ±0.02 mm
Position Accuracy ±0.1 mm
Duty Cycle 24/7 operation capability

4. Environmental Specifications

  • Operating Temperature: 5°C to 40°C
  • Storage Temperature: -10°C to 55°C
  • Humidity: 20% to 80% non-condensing
  • Protection Class: IP54 (optional IP65)
  • Power Supply: 200-240VAC, 50/60Hz, 3.2kW max

5. Safety Features

  • ISO/TS 15066 compliant
  • Category 3, PLd safety rated
  • Integrated collision detection
  • Safe force and speed monitoring
  • Emergency stop circuits
