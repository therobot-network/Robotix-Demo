Set Up Automatic Replies (Out of Office)

Summary

Automatic replies (also called "Out of Office" or OOO) let senders know you are away and when you will return. You can configure separate messages for people inside Robotix Technologies and for external contacts. Automatic replies can be scheduled in advance with a start and end date and time.

Before You Begin

  • You can set up automatic replies from any version of Outlook (Windows, macOS, Web, or mobile).
  • Outlook on the Web is the most reliable method if you are setting up replies close to your departure time, as changes take effect immediately without requiring a sync.
  • If you manage a shared mailbox, automatic replies for that mailbox must be configured by a Service Desk administrator — submit a request at https://help.robotix.com.

Steps

Outlook on Windows

  1. Open Outlook and click File in the ribbon.
  2. Click Automatic Replies (Out of Office).
  3. Select Send automatic replies.
  4. Check Only send during this time range and set your Start time and End time using the date/time pickers. This prevents the reply from sending indefinitely if you forget to turn it off.
  5. On the Inside My Organization tab, type your internal message. Example:

    "Thank you for your email. I am out of the office from [date] through [date] and will have limited access to email. For urgent matters, please contact [colleague name] at [colleague email] or reach the Robotix Service Desk at servicedesk@robotix.com."

  6. Click the Outside My Organization tab and check Auto-reply to people outside my organization.
  7. Choose Anyone outside my organization or My Contacts only based on your preference.
  8. Type your external message (you may use a shorter version that omits internal colleague contacts).
  9. Click OK to save. A yellow bar will appear in Outlook confirming automatic replies are active.

Outlook on macOS

  1. Open Outlook and go to Tools > Automatic Replies in the menu bar.
  2. Check Send automatic replies for account and select your Robotix IT account from the dropdown if you have multiple accounts.
  3. Check Send replies only during this time period and set your start and end dates.
  4. Type your message for Reply to senders inside your organization.
  5. Type a separate message for Reply to senders outside your organization if desired.
  6. Click OK.

Outlook on the Web (Recommended for Quick Setup)

  1. Go to https://outlook.office.com and sign in via Okta.
  2. Click the Settings gear in the top-right corner.
  3. In the Settings panel, search for "automatic replies" and select Automatic replies.
  4. Toggle Automatic replies on to the on position.
  5. Check Send replies only during a time period and enter your start and end date/time.
  6. In the text box under Send a reply inside your organization, write your internal message.
  7. Check Send replies outside your organization and choose Contact list only or Anyone from the dropdown.
  8. Write your external message in the text box provided.
  9. Click Save at the top of the panel.

Outlook Mobile (iOS / Android)

  1. Open Outlook on your mobile device.
  2. Tap your profile picture in the top-left corner.
  3. Tap the Settings gear at the bottom of the account panel.
  4. Scroll down and tap your Robotix IT account.
  5. Tap Automatic Replies.
  6. Toggle Automatic Replies on and type your message.
  7. Toggle Reply to everyone or Reply to my organization only as needed.
  8. Tap the back arrow to save — the setting takes effect immediately.

Turn Off Automatic Replies Early

If you return before your scheduled end date, turn off automatic replies manually: - Windows/macOS: File > Automatic Replies > select Do not send automatic replies > OK. - Web: Settings > Automatic replies > toggle off > Save. - Mobile: Settings > your account > Automatic Replies > toggle off.

Troubleshooting

Symptom Action
Senders report they are not receiving your OOO Confirm "Outside my organization" replies are turned on and the sender is not in a blocked sender list.
Automatic replies are not stopping at the scheduled end time Manually turn off the reply after returning; verify time zone settings match your local time zone.
"Automatic Replies" option is greyed out on macOS Ensure you are signed in to the correct account (not just a local/cached account). Try setting up via the Web client instead.
OOO fired immediately for a future date Check that your PC/Mac clock and time zone are set correctly — Outlook uses the local system clock.
