Set Up Robotix Email on Your iPhone or Android (Outlook Mobile)

Summary

Microsoft Outlook for iOS and Android is the supported mobile email client for Robotix Technologies. This article explains how to install and configure Outlook on your personal or company-issued mobile device so you can access your Robotix IT email, calendar, and contacts on the go. Your account authenticates through Okta SSO.

Before You Begin

  • Your mobile device must meet minimum OS requirements: iOS 16 or later, or Android 10 or later.
  • If you are setting up a company-issued device, it may already be enrolled in Intune. Follow the Company Portal enrollment prompts before adding your email account.
  • Have your Robotix IT credentials ready: firstname.lastname@robotix.com and your Okta password.
  • Okta Verify must be installed on the same device (or another enrolled device) to approve MFA prompts.
  • Personal devices: You may be prompted to allow Intune mobile device management (MDM) policies. This is required to access Robotix IT email and only enforces security policies (PIN, encryption); it does not give IT access to personal data.

Steps

iPhone (iOS)

  1. Open the App Store and search for Microsoft Outlook. Tap Get to download and install it (it's free).
  2. Open Outlook. Tap Add Account if this is a fresh install, or go to Settings (gear icon) > Add Account > Add Email Account if you already have another account in the app.
  3. Enter your Robotix IT email address (firstname.lastname@robotix.com) and tap Add Account.
  4. You will be redirected to the Okta sign-in page. Enter your Okta password and tap Sign In.
  5. Approve the Okta Verify push notification.
  6. If prompted to enroll your device in mobile device management, tap Enroll and follow the Company Portal prompts. This step is required for corporate email access.
  7. Grant Outlook permission to send you notifications when asked — this enables new mail alerts.
  8. Tap Maybe Later or Skip on the "Add another account" screen when your Robotix IT mailbox appears.

Android

  1. Open the Google Play Store and search for Microsoft Outlook. Tap Install.
  2. Open Outlook and tap Get Started, then Add Account.
  3. Enter firstname.lastname@robotix.com and tap Continue.
  4. Outlook will open the Okta sign-in page. Enter your password and tap Sign In.
  5. Approve the Okta Verify push notification on your enrolled device.
  6. If prompted, install and open Microsoft Intune Company Portal from the Play Store to complete device enrollment. Follow the on-screen steps, then return to Outlook.
  7. Allow notification permissions when prompted.
  8. Your mailbox will begin syncing. Tap Calendar at the bottom to verify your Robotix IT calendar appears.

Configure Key Settings (Both Platforms)

  • Notifications: Go to Settings > Notifications within Outlook and enable alerts for your inbox.
  • Focused Inbox: Tap Settings > Focused Inbox to toggle. Focused Inbox filters high-priority messages; disable it if you prefer a unified view.
  • Sync period: Under Settings > your account, set Sync mail from to 1 month or 3 months based on preference.

Troubleshooting

Symptom Action
"Your organization requires a device policy" error Install Company Portal and complete Intune enrollment, then reopen Outlook.
MFA push not arriving Open Okta Verify and check your enrolled devices list; re-enroll if the device is missing.
Email syncs but calendar is empty In Outlook Settings, tap your account and ensure Calendar sync is enabled.
"Cannot connect to server" after correct login Toggle airplane mode off and on; check that mobile data or Wi-Fi is active.
Outlook not receiving push notifications on iOS Go to iPhone Settings > Notifications > Outlook and ensure Allow Notifications is on.
