Set Up Okta Verify Multi-Factor Authentication

Summary

Robotix IT requires multi-factor authentication (MFA) on all accounts. Okta Verify is the standard MFA app — it delivers a push notification to your phone that you tap to approve each sign-in. This article walks you through installing Okta Verify and enrolling it with your Robotix account for the first time.

Before You Begin

  • A smartphone running iOS 16 or later, or Android 10 or later.
  • Your Robotix credentials (firstname.lastname@robotix.com and your current password).
  • A stable internet connection on both your computer and your phone.
  • If you are setting up MFA for the first time, Okta will prompt you automatically during your first login — you can follow the same steps below starting at Step 4.

Steps

Part 1 — Install Okta Verify on Your Phone

  1. On your smartphone, open the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android).
  2. Search for Okta Verify (published by Okta, Inc.) and tap Install / Get.
  3. Open the app once installation is complete. Accept any notification permissions the app requests — push notifications are required for the approve/deny flow.

Part 2 — Enroll Your Device in Okta

  1. On your computer, sign in at https://robotix.okta.com with your email and password.
  2. If this is your first login or MFA is not yet enrolled, Okta presents "Set up multifactor authentication." Click Set up next to Okta Verify. - If you are adding Okta Verify to an already-enrolled account: click your name in the top-right corner → Settings → scroll to Extra Verification → click Set up next to Okta Verify.
  3. Choose your phone's platform: iPhone or Android. Click Next.
  4. Okta displays a QR code on screen.
  5. In the Okta Verify app on your phone, tap the + (Add Account) button.
  6. Tap Organization, then tap Yes, Ready to Scan.
  7. Point your phone's camera at the QR code on your computer screen. The app scans it automatically.
  8. Okta Verify displays "Account added" and your Robotix email address appears in the app.
  9. Back on your computer, click Done.

Part 3 — Test Your Setup

  1. Sign out of Okta (click your name → Sign out).
  2. Sign back in with your email and password.
  3. When prompted, approve the push notification that appears on your phone within 60 seconds.
  4. You should land on your Okta dashboard — MFA is working correctly.

Troubleshooting

Symptom Likely Cause Fix
QR code not scanning Camera permissions not granted to Okta Verify Go to phone Settings → Apps → Okta Verify → Permissions → allow Camera
Push notification never arrives Phone notifications for Okta Verify are blocked Go to phone Settings → Notifications → Okta Verify → turn on Allow Notifications
"Account already exists" in the app Previous enrollment still active Remove the old account in the app (swipe left on iOS, long-press on Android) and re-scan
QR code expires before scanning QR codes expire after 5 minutes Click Refresh on the Okta page to get a new QR code
Can't find "Extra Verification" in Okta settings Your admin policy may restrict self-enrollment Submit a request at https://help.robotix.com to have IT enroll you
