Request Access to a Shared Mailbox

Summary

Shared mailboxes in Microsoft 365 let teams receive and send email from a common address (e.g., billing@robotix.com or hr-support@robotix.com). If you need access to one of these mailboxes — either to read, send, or both — your manager must approve the request, which IT then provisions in Exchange Online.

Before You Begin

  • Know the full email address of the shared mailbox you need access to. If you are unsure of the exact address, check with your team or the mailbox owner.
  • Confirm with your manager that they are willing to approve this access — the request form sends an approval email to your manager automatically.
  • Decide whether you need read-only access (view and reply from your own account) or full access (read, send as the mailbox, and manage the mailbox content).
  • If you need to be the mailbox owner or delegate manager (someone who can add/remove other members), note this in your request — it requires an additional approval step.

Steps

Submitting the Request

  1. Log in to https://help.robotix.com.
  2. Go to Service Catalog → Accounts & Access → Shared Mailbox Access Request.
  3. Fill in the form: - Shared Mailbox Address: Enter the full address (e.g., finance-inbox@robotix.com). If you do not know the exact address, enter a description and IT will locate it. - Access Level: Choose Full Access (read + send as) or Read Only. - Business Justification: Briefly explain why you need access (e.g., "I am covering for the Finance team while Jane is on leave"). - Manager: Pre-populated from your profile. If you are requesting on behalf of someone else, adjust this field.
  4. Click Submit. Your manager receives an approval email from RobotixDesk.
  5. Once your manager approves (they click Approve in the email), IT provisions access within 1 business day. You receive a confirmation email when it is ready.

Adding the Shared Mailbox in Outlook (Windows)

  1. Open Outlook on your PC.
  2. Right-click your account name in the left sidebar and select Add Shared Folder or Mailbox.
  3. Type the shared mailbox address and click Add.
  4. The mailbox appears below your personal mailbox in the sidebar. You can expand it to see Inbox, Sent Items, and other folders.

Adding the Shared Mailbox in Outlook (macOS)

  1. Open Outlook for Mac.
  2. In the menu bar, click File → Open → Other User's Folder.
  3. Search for the shared mailbox name or email address and click Open.
  4. Alternatively, the mailbox may auto-mount within a few minutes of provisioning — check your account list in the sidebar.

Accessing via Outlook Web App

  1. Go to https://outlook.office365.com and sign in.
  2. Right-click your name in the left sidebar and select Add shared folder.
  3. Enter the mailbox address and click Add.

Sending Mail as the Shared Mailbox

  • In the compose window, click From and select the shared mailbox address.
  • If the From field is not visible, click the three dots (…) menu at the top of the compose window and choose Show From.
  • Replies to emails sent from the shared mailbox land in the shared mailbox inbox, not your personal inbox.

Troubleshooting

Symptom Likely Cause Fix
Shared mailbox does not appear after provisioning confirmation Outlook cache not refreshed Restart Outlook or right-click and remove/re-add the shared folder
"You don't have permission to open this mailbox" Provisioning may still be propagating in Exchange Online Wait up to 1 hour after IT confirms provisioning and try again
Cannot see the "From" field to send as the mailbox Outlook display setting hidden In Outlook: Options → From → Show From
Manager did not receive approval email Email went to spam, or wrong manager on file Ask your manager to check spam; contact IT to resend the approval request
Request form shows "No mailbox found" for the address Mailbox name is slightly different from what you entered Try a partial search or contact the Service Desk to look up the correct address
