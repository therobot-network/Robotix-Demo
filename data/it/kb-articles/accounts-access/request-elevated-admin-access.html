Request Elevated or Administrator Access

Summary

Elevated and administrator access grants permissions beyond those included in your standard role — for example, local admin rights on a device, an Okta admin role, Microsoft 365 admin center access, or infrastructure-level permissions. These requests require manager approval, a business justification, and a security review, and they are subject to periodic access recertification.

Before You Begin

  • Elevated access is never granted by default. You must have a clear, documented business need.
  • Your direct manager and their skip-level manager (for admin-level roles) must approve. The form sends approval requests automatically.
  • Some role types require Security Operations (SOC) review before IT can provision access. This can add 2–5 business days to the timeline.
  • If you need temporary elevated access (e.g., for a project lasting under 30 days), request time-limited access — this is faster to approve and automatically revokes itself.
  • All privileged accounts are subject to quarterly access recertification. You will receive a quarterly email asking you to confirm you still need the access. Failure to respond within 10 business days results in automatic revocation.
  • Privileged accounts require a YubiKey security key as an additional MFA factor. If you do not have one, IT will issue it as part of the provisioning process.

Steps

  1. Go to https://help.robotix.com.
  2. Navigate to Service Catalog → Accounts & Access → Elevated / Administrator Access Request.
  3. In the Access Type drop-down, select the category that matches your need:
Access Type Description
Local Admin — Windows Full local admin on your assigned Windows 11 laptop
Local Admin — macOS Admin rights on your assigned MacBook
Okta Admin Access to the Okta Admin Console (read-only, help desk, or super admin)
Microsoft 365 Admin Access to M365 Admin Center, Exchange Admin, or SharePoint Admin
Azure / Entra Admin Azure portal or Microsoft Entra ID management roles
AWS Console AWS IAM role assignment for specific accounts/environments
RobotixDesk Admin RobotixDesk ITSM queue management or admin panel
Other (describe below) For anything not listed — describe in the justification field
  1. Fill in Scope and Duration: - Scope: What specific resources or permissions do you need? (e.g., "Read-only access to the Okta admin console to assist with L1 help desk tasks") - Duration: Permanent, or time-limited with an end date.
  2. Fill in Business Justification: A clear 2–4 sentence explanation of why you need this access and what business function it supports.
  3. Under Approvers, confirm your direct manager is listed. For Okta admin, M365 admin, or Azure roles, the form automatically adds the IT Director as a required approver.
  4. Acknowledge the Acceptable Use Policy for privileged accounts by checking the confirmation box. This indicates you have read the policy at https://help.robotix.com/policies/privileged-access.
  5. Click Submit. Your ticket number appears in the format REQ-######.

After Submission

  • Manager approval is typically received within 1 business day.
  • Requests requiring SOC review are escalated to L3 Engineering and may take up to 5 business days.
  • Once all approvals are collected, IT provisions the access and emails you with the next steps, including YubiKey issuance if needed.
  • If you requested local admin on Windows, the change is pushed via Microsoft Intune and takes effect after the next Intune sync (up to 2 hours) or a reboot.
  • If you requested local admin on macOS, the change is pushed via Jamf Pro within 15 minutes.

Troubleshooting

Symptom Likely Cause Fix
Manager approval email not received Email in spam, or wrong manager listed in your HR profile Ask manager to check spam; contact IT to resend or correct the manager field
Access type not in the drop-down list Non-standard or new system Select "Other (describe below)" and describe the access in detail
Request denied by SOC Violation of least-privilege policy or missing justification Contact your manager and the Service Desk to discuss an alternative scoped request
Local admin granted but not working Intune/Jamf policy has not synced yet Windows: open Company Portal → sync; Mac: open System Preferences → Profiles; or restart the device
Quarterly recertification email arrived but access is still needed Expected — just respond "Confirm" Click the Confirm Access link in the email within 10 business days
Access was auto-revoked after recertification deadline No response to recertification email Resubmit the access request from Step 1
