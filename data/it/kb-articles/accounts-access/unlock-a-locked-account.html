Unlock a Locked Account

Summary

Your Robotix account locks automatically after five consecutive failed sign-in attempts. In most cases you can unlock yourself through Okta's self-service flow without waiting for the Service Desk. If self-service is unavailable, the Service Desk can unlock your account in minutes.

Before You Begin

  • Know which email address is associated with your account (firstname.lastname@robotix.com).
  • Have access to a registered recovery method — either the Okta Verify app on your phone, your personal recovery email address, or your security question answers.
  • If you believe your account may have been locked by a security policy (repeated failures from an unusual location), contact the Service Desk rather than attempting self-unlock, so the team can review the activity first.

Steps

Option A — Self-Service Unlock via Okta

  1. Browse to https://robotix.okta.com.
  2. Attempt to sign in. If your account is locked, Okta displays: "Your account is locked. Please contact your administrator or click here to unlock." Click the click here to unlock link.
  3. Enter your @robotix.com email address and click Next.
  4. Select a verification method to prove your identity: - Okta Verify push notification — tap Approve in the Okta Verify app. - Email verification code — retrieve the 6-digit code sent to your recovery email and enter it on screen. - Security question — type the answer exactly as you entered it during setup.
  5. After successful verification, Okta displays: "Your account is now unlocked."
  6. Click Back to sign in and log in normally.
  7. On a Windows device, lock (Win + L) and unlock your screen to refresh the local credential cache if prompted for a password.

Option B — Contact the Service Desk

If you do not have a registered recovery method, or the self-service link does not appear, submit a ticket or contact us directly:

  1. Visit https://help.robotix.com and submit a "Locked Account" request — you can do this from any device including personal.
  2. Alternatively, call +1 (800) 555-7866 or message #it-help in Slack.
  3. An L1 agent will verify your identity (manager confirmation or employee ID + last four of SSN) and unlock your account within 15 minutes during staffed hours (7 AM – 7 PM ET, Mon–Fri).

Troubleshooting

Symptom Likely Cause Fix
"click here to unlock" link does not appear Account is suspended (not just locked) or this is a first-time login Contact the Service Desk — suspension requires admin action
Verification methods are greyed out No recovery factors enrolled Service Desk unlock only; enroll recovery methods after regaining access
Account unlocks but locks again immediately Saved incorrect password in a browser, email app, or mobile device Update saved passwords in all apps before signing back in
Windows Hello PIN or fingerprint rejected after unlock Windows Hello credential bound to previous session Sign in with password once; Windows Hello will re-enroll automatically
