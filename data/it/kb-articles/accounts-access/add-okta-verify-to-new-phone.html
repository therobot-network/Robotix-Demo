Add Okta Verify to a New Phone or Move MFA to a New Device

Summary

When you get a new phone, upgrade your operating system on a fresh install, or replace a lost or broken device, you need to re-enroll Okta Verify so you can continue to approve sign-in requests. This article covers how to add a new phone to your existing account — with or without access to your old device.

Before You Begin

  • Know your Robotix email address (firstname.lastname@robotix.com) and password.
  • If you still have your old phone: you can self-serve the re-enrollment directly from your Okta account settings.
  • If you do not have your old phone (lost, broken, traded in): you will need the Service Desk to temporarily bypass MFA so you can enroll a new device. Plan for a 15–30 minute call to verify your identity.
  • Do not factory-reset or trade in your old phone until Okta Verify is working on your new one.

Steps

Scenario A — You Still Have Your Old Phone

  1. Install Okta Verify on your new phone from the App Store or Google Play Store.
  2. On your computer, go to https://robotix.okta.com and sign in using your email, password, and the push notification on your old phone to complete MFA.
  3. Click your name in the upper-right corner, then click Settings.
  4. Scroll down to the Extra Verification section.
  5. Under Okta Verify, click Set up another (or Add another — the label varies by version).
  6. Select your new phone's platform (iPhone or Android) and click Next.
  7. A QR code is displayed on screen.
  8. On your new phone, open the Okta Verify app and tap + → Organization → Yes, Ready to Scan.
  9. Scan the QR code. The app confirms "Account added."
  10. Verify the new device works: sign out of Okta and sign back in — approve the push on your new phone.
  11. Once confirmed working, remove the old device from Okta: return to Settings → Extra Verification → Okta Verify, find the old device entry (it shows the device name and enrollment date), and click Remove.

Scenario B — You Do Not Have Your Old Phone

  1. Install Okta Verify on your new phone.
  2. Contact the Robotix Service Desk: - Call +1 (800) 555-7866, email servicedesk@robotix.com, or open a ticket at https://help.robotix.com. - Select the request type "MFA Device Reset."
  3. An agent will verify your identity — typically via manager confirmation or a video call with government-issued ID.
  4. The agent will temporarily remove your old MFA enrollment, which lets you complete a fresh enrollment using the self-service flow.
  5. You will receive an email prompt to set up a new MFA factor. Follow the on-screen Okta enrollment steps (identical to KB-0102, Steps 4–12).
  6. After successful enrollment, the agent will close the ticket and your new device is active.

Security note: MFA resets are a common social engineering target. The Service Desk will never reset MFA based solely on an email request — voice or video identity verification is always required.

Troubleshooting

Symptom Likely Cause Fix
New phone not receiving push notifications Notification permissions denied or phone is in Do Not Disturb Check Settings → Notifications → Okta Verify; disable DND
"Maximum devices enrolled" error Account already has the maximum number of enrolled devices (typically 5) Remove an unused old device from Okta Settings → Extra Verification
QR code scanned but account doesn't appear in app Network issue during enrollment Delete the partial account in the app and re-scan
Agent says they cannot reset MFA without manager approval Policy requirement for identity verification Ask your direct manager to email servicedesk@robotix.com confirming the request
