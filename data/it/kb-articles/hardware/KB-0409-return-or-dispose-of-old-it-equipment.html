Return or Dispose of Old IT Equipment

Summary

This article explains the process for returning Robotix IT-issued equipment that is no longer needed — including laptops, monitors, docks, keyboards, mice, and headsets — due to an upgrade, offboarding, role change, or office closure. You must never dispose of Robotix IT equipment in general trash or household recycling. All devices contain sensitive data and must be returned to IT for certified data destruction and responsible e-waste recycling.

Before You Begin

  • Do not delete files or wipe your device before returning it. IT performs certified data erasure as part of the decommission process; wiping it yourself may interfere with asset tracking or compliance logging.
  • Gather all accessories that were issued with the device: power adapter, USB-C cable, dock, keyboard, mouse, headset, and any carry case.
  • You will need your device's asset tag (SUM-LT-#####, SUM-MB-#####, SUM-MN-#####, etc.). If the label is missing or unreadable, include the device's serial number (found under Settings > System > About on Windows, or Apple menu > About This Mac on macOS).
  • If you are an offboarding employee, your manager or HR must initiate the offboarding process in Workday before equipment return — IT will receive an automated task linked to your end date.

Steps

For Current Employees (Device Upgrade or Equipment No Longer Needed)

  1. Open the Robotix Service Desk portal at https://help.robotix.com and sign in with your Okta credentials.
  2. Click Request a Service and select Hardware & Equipment > Equipment Return / Disposal.
  3. Fill in all required fields: - Asset tag(s): Enter the asset tag for each device being returned. Add multiple tags if returning more than one item. - Return reason: Upgrade/Refresh, Role Change, Duplicate Equipment, or Other. - Return method: Select Drop-off at office IT room, IT pickup at my desk (available at select offices), or Ship to IT (remote employees). - Data on device: Confirm whether you have personal files stored locally that you need to transfer before return. If yes, IT will schedule a data transfer session.
  4. Click Submit. You will receive a confirmation (format: REQ-######).
  5. Sync any remaining work files to OneDrive or SharePoint before the return date. Do not copy company files to personal storage (personal USB drives, personal cloud accounts).
  6. Sign out of all personal accounts (e.g., personal browser profiles) on the device before handoff. Do not factory-reset the device.
  7. On the agreed return date, package the device and all accessories securely. IT will provide prepaid shipping labels for remote employees.
  8. Hand off the device to the IT technician or ship it using the provided label. You will receive an email confirmation when IT checks in the asset and removes it from your name in the asset register.

For Offboarding Employees

  1. Your manager should notify HR of your departure in Workday at least 5 business days before your last day. IT receives an automated task from this workflow.
  2. IT will contact you before your last day to schedule equipment collection. If you have not heard from IT within 2 business days of your end date being set, contact the service desk directly.
  3. Prepare all Robotix IT-issued equipment for return: - Laptops, monitors, docks, keyboards, mice, headsets, and any other Robotix IT-tagged items. - Company credit cards, access cards, or office keys are returned to HR/Facilities — not IT.
  4. Do not attempt to access company systems after your account is deactivated. Okta access is revoked at the end of business on your last day.
  5. Confirm with your manager that all necessary file handoff (shared drives, project files) has been completed before your last day.

Troubleshooting

Issue Action
Asset tag label is missing from the device Provide the device serial number instead (Settings > System > About on Windows; Apple menu > About This Mac on macOS)
Personal files still on the device at handoff Inform the IT technician before handing over the device. IT can schedule a brief data transfer session; they will not access your personal files without your presence
Received a prepaid shipping label but it won't print Contact the service desk for a new label; do not pay for your own shipping
Equipment was disposed of before contacting IT Report this to your manager and IT immediately. Unauthorized disposal of IT assets may require an incident report for compliance purposes
Former employee equipment not yet collected weeks after offboarding The manager should open a ticket on the portal requesting IT to collect the equipment
