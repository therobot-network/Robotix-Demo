Report a Lost or Stolen Mobile Device

Summary

If your mobile device is lost or stolen, you must report it to Robotix IT immediately. IT can remotely lock the device, locate it (on company-owned devices), and selectively wipe corporate data to prevent unauthorized access to Robotix systems. Speed is critical — act as soon as you realize the device is missing.

Before You Begin

  • This process applies to any device — company-issued or personal BYOD — that is enrolled in Intune and has access to Robotix email, Teams, or other corporate applications.
  • You do not need to have the device in hand to report it.
  • If you believe the device was stolen rather than lost, also file a police report and note the report number — you may need it for HR or insurance purposes.
  • Robotix IT will take the following actions depending on device type:
  • Company-issued device: Remote lock + full wipe after IT assessment.
  • BYOD device: Selective wipe — only Robotix corporate data and apps are removed. Personal photos, apps, and data are not affected.

Steps

Immediate Actions (Do These Now)

  1. Call the Robotix Service Desk immediately at +1 (800) 555-7866. This is the fastest way to trigger an emergency remote lock. Service Desk is staffed 7 AM–7 PM ET Monday–Friday; after hours, call the same number and select option 1 for the P1 on-call line.
  2. Alternatively, submit a high-priority ticket at https://help.robotix.com: - Click Report Lost/Stolen Device from the home page. - Fill in: device type, make and model, asset tag (if company-issued), last known location, date and approximate time last seen. - Set Priority to P2 – High.
  3. Change your Robotix password immediately at https://okta.robotix.com > My Account > Change Password. This invalidates active Okta sessions on the lost device.
  4. If you have another phone or computer, open Okta Dashboard, click your name, and select Sign Out All Sessions to terminate any lingering sessions tied to the lost device.

After Reporting — What IT Will Do

  1. IT will issue a remote lock within 15 minutes of receiving your report, requiring a PIN to unlock the device.
  2. For company-issued devices, IT may initiate a remote GPS locate (iOS Find My / Android Find My Device) if enabled. Share the tracking result with IT if you have access to it from another device.
  3. IT will schedule a remote wipe if the device cannot be recovered within 24 hours or if you confirm it is stolen.
  4. IT will remove the device from your Intune enrollment and revoke its access certificates.
  5. You will receive a ticket update confirming each action taken.

If You Find the Device After Reporting

  1. Contact the Service Desk immediately at +1 (800) 555-7866 or via Slack #it-help.
  2. Do not attempt to use the device until IT clears it — remote wipe may still be pending.
  3. IT will verify device integrity before restoring access and may require the device to be re-enrolled in Intune.

Replacing a Company-Issued Device

  1. Submit a Hardware Replacement Request at https://help.robotix.com after reporting the loss.
  2. A replacement will be provisioned from IT inventory. Standard lead time is 1–2 business days.
  3. IT will restore apps and settings from your Intune configuration profile. Personal files stored in OneDrive are unaffected.

Troubleshooting

Situation What to do
Cannot reach Service Desk by phone Email servicedesk@robotix.com with URGENT in the subject line, or message in Slack #it-help from another device.
Cannot change your password (forgot it) Call the Service Desk — agents can initiate an emergency password reset with identity verification over the phone.
Device is signed out of Robotix apps but not wiped An Intune conditional access policy may have blocked it. Remote wipe is still needed — contact IT to confirm.
Personal data concern after wipe request BYOD selective wipe only removes Robotix app data (Outlook, Teams, Company Portal). Personal photos, messages, and apps are not touched. See KB-0610 for details.
