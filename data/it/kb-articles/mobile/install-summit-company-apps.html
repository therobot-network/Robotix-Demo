Install Robotix Company Apps on Your Mobile Device

Summary

Once your device is enrolled in Intune, you can install Robotix-approved apps through the Company Portal app (similar to an internal app store). This article explains how to browse and install required and optional Robotix apps on your iOS or Android device.

Before You Begin

  • Your device must be enrolled in Intune via Company Portal before apps can be installed. See KB-0605 if you have not enrolled yet.
  • Some apps are required and will install automatically after enrollment. Others are optional and you install on demand.
  • Company Portal on iOS uses Managed Apps — these apps are licensed through Robotix's Microsoft 365 and Apple VPP agreement. You are not charged individually for them.
  • On Android, apps install into your work profile (the briefcase-icon section of your app drawer). Work apps and personal apps remain separate.

Steps

Browse and Install Apps via Company Portal (iOS)

  1. Open Company Portal on your iPhone or iPad.
  2. Tap Apps at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Browse the Featured list or tap All Apps to see the full catalog.
  4. Required apps (marked with a red badge) are pushed automatically — you may see a prompt to install them.
  5. To install an optional app, tap its name and then tap Install.
  6. If prompted to trust or allow the app management profile, tap Install again.
  7. The app appears on your home screen or in a Robotix apps folder when installation is complete.

Browse and Install Apps via Company Portal (Android)

  1. In your app drawer, look for the work profile section (apps with a briefcase icon overlay).
  2. Open Company Portal from the work profile section.
  3. Tap Apps in the bottom navigation bar.
  4. Browse or search for the app you need. Tap the app name, then tap Install.
  5. The app installs into the work profile — it appears in the work profile section of your app drawer with the briefcase icon, separate from your personal apps.

Install a Required App That Did Not Auto-Install

  1. Open Company Portal and tap Devices.
  2. Tap your device name.
  3. If a compliance warning appears listing a missing required app, tap Resolve.
  4. Company Portal will attempt to push the required app. Alternatively, navigate to the Apps tab and install it manually using the steps above.

Troubleshooting

Symptom What to try
App does not appear in Company Portal catalog Not all apps are available on all platforms. Confirm you are on iOS or Android — some apps are Windows-only and appear in Company Portal on your laptop instead.
"Installation failed" error Check available storage on your device (need at least 500 MB free). Also ensure you are on Wi-Fi for large downloads.
App installed but not signed into Robotix account Open the app and sign in with firstname.lastname@robotix.com. The first sign-in will redirect through Okta.
Android: app missing from work profile Installed work apps appear only in the work profile section of your drawer, not in the personal section. Swipe to find the briefcase-icon area.
iOS: "Unable to download app" or App Store prompts for personal Apple ID Company Portal installs managed apps without requiring your personal Apple ID. If prompted, tap Cancel and re-initiate the install from Company Portal, not the App Store directly.
