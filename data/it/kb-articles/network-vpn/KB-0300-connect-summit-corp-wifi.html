Connect to the Robotix-Corp Corporate Wi-Fi

Summary

Robotix IT's corporate wireless network uses the SSID Robotix-Corp. It runs WPA2-Enterprise (802.1X) authentication, which means you sign in with your standard Okta credentials — the same username and password you use to log in to your computer and company applications. This article walks you through connecting on all supported platforms.

Before You Begin

  • You must have an active Robotix IT user account (firstname.lastname@robotix.com).
  • Your device must be enrolled in Microsoft Intune (Windows) or Jamf Pro (Mac/iOS). Unenrolled personal devices cannot authenticate to Robotix-Corp; use Robotix-Guest instead.
  • Have your Okta credentials ready. If you do not know your password, reset it at https://help.robotix.com before proceeding.
  • Okta Verify must be installed on your phone or device for MFA prompts.

Steps

Windows 11

  1. Click the Wi-Fi icon in the system tray (bottom-right of the taskbar).
  2. In the network flyout, locate Robotix-Corp in the list of available networks and click it.
  3. Click Connect.
  4. In the authentication dialog, enter: - Username: firstname.lastname@robotix.com - Password: Your Okta password
  5. If prompted to verify the server certificate, confirm the certificate is issued to radius.robotix.com and click Connect.
  6. Approve the Okta Verify push notification on your phone.
  7. Windows will show Connected, secured under Robotix-Corp when the connection is established.

Tip: Check Connect automatically before clicking Connect so your laptop rejoins Robotix-Corp every time you enter the office.

macOS (Sonoma / Sequoia)

  1. Click the Wi-Fi menu (top-right menu bar) and select Robotix-Corp.
  2. In the authentication sheet that appears, enter: - Username: firstname.lastname@robotix.com - Password: Your Okta password
  3. Click Join.
  4. If a certificate trust dialog appears, click Continue after confirming the certificate is issued to radius.robotix.com.
  5. Approve the Okta Verify push notification on your phone.
  6. A checkmark appears next to Robotix-Corp in the Wi-Fi menu when connected.

iOS (iPhone / iPad)

  1. Open Settings > Wi-Fi.
  2. Tap Robotix-Corp under available networks.
  3. Enter: - Username: firstname.lastname@robotix.com - Password: Your Okta password
  4. Tap Join.
  5. If a certificate prompt appears, tap Trust.
  6. Approve the Okta Verify push notification.
  7. A checkmark and Wi-Fi bars appear next to Robotix-Corp when connected.

Android

  1. Open Settings > Network & internet > Wi-Fi.
  2. Tap Robotix-Corp.
  3. Configure the following fields: - EAP method: PEAP - Phase 2 authentication: MSCHAPV2 - CA certificate: Use system certificates (or select radius.robotix.com if listed) - Domain: robotix.com - Identity: firstname.lastname@robotix.com - Password: Your Okta password
  4. Tap Connect.
  5. Approve the Okta Verify push notification.

Troubleshooting

Symptom Likely Cause Fix
"Authentication failed" or "Wrong password" Incorrect Okta credentials Reset your password at https://help.robotix.com
Robotix-Corp not visible in the network list Too far from an access point, or AP issue Move closer to a Robotix IT wireless access point; contact the Service Desk if it still doesn't appear
Stuck on "Connecting…" for more than 30 seconds MFA push not approved Check your phone for an Okta Verify push notification and tap Approve
Certificate warning or untrusted certificate Device clock is wrong, or wrong network Confirm date/time is correct; ensure you are connecting to Robotix-Corp, not a spoofed SSID
Android shows "Connected" but no internet Wrong EAP / Phase 2 settings Re-enter the Android settings exactly as listed above; toggle Wi-Fi off and back on
Mac repeatedly asks for credentials macOS keychain conflict Open Keychain Access, search for Robotix-Corp, delete any old entries, then reconnect
Device is not enrolled in Intune/Jamf Personal or unenrolled device Use Robotix-Guest for internet access; contact the Service Desk to enroll your corporate device
