Scan to Email from a Multifunction Printer

Summary

All Robotix HP and Xerox multifunction printers (MFPs) support scanning documents directly to your Robotix email address. This article explains how to scan a single page or multi-page document and have the result delivered to your Outlook inbox as a PDF or TIFF attachment.

Before You Begin

  • You must have a Robotix IT account (firstname.lastname@robotix.com).
  • The MFP must be online — check that the front panel displays a ready state (no error lights).
  • Scan-to-email routes through the Robotix mail relay. Scans arrive in your inbox within 1–3 minutes under normal conditions.
  • Maximum scan job size is 50 pages per session. For larger documents, split into multiple jobs.
  • Supported output formats: PDF (default) and TIFF. PDF is recommended for most purposes.

Steps

Scan to Your Own Email Address

  1. Place your document face-up in the automatic document feeder (ADF) or face-down on the flatbed glass, aligned to the corner guides.
  2. On the printer's touchscreen, tap Scan (HP) or Services > Email (Xerox).
  3. Sign in when prompted: - Tap your account if shown, or enter your Robotix username and password. - On badge-enabled printers, tap your proximity badge to sign in automatically.
  4. The To: field is pre-populated with your own email address. Confirm it is correct.
  5. (Optional) Tap the Subject or File Name field to enter a descriptive name for the scan (e.g., Contract-SignedCopy).
  6. Select your desired settings: - Resolution: 200 dpi (standard) or 300 dpi (detailed documents, fine print). - Color mode: Color, Grayscale, or Black & White. - File type: PDF or TIFF. - Sides: 1-sided or 2-sided (duplex) scanning.
  7. Tap Send or Start Scan.
  8. The printer feeds and scans the pages. A confirmation message appears on screen and you receive an email with the attachment in 1–3 minutes.

Scan to a Colleague's Email Address

  1. Follow steps 1–3 above to sign in and reach the scan screen.
  2. Tap the To: field.
  3. Type the recipient's name or @robotix.com address. The printer looks up Robotix directory entries — tap the correct name in the results.
  4. To add multiple recipients, tap Add after each address.
  5. Complete steps 5–8 above.

Scan from the Flatbed (Single Sheet / Book / ID Card)

  1. Lift the printer lid and place the item face-down on the glass, aligned to the corner arrows.
  2. Lower the lid gently.
  3. Follow the scan-to-email steps above. On the scan settings screen, ensure Flatbed is selected (not ADF) if the printer does not auto-detect.
  4. Tap Send. The printer will scan the flatbed area only.

Troubleshooting

Symptom What to try
Scan email not received after 5 minutes Check your Junk/Spam folder. If not there, confirm you are signed into the correct account on the printer.
"Authentication required" on the printer screen Your session timed out. Sign in again using your Robotix credentials or badge.
Pages are skewed or cut off Reposition pages in the ADF aligned to the guides, or use the flatbed for irregularly sized documents.
Multi-page job arrives as separate files Select Combine to Single File or Multi-Page PDF in the scan settings before sending.
ADF jams mid-scan Open the ADF cover, remove the jammed pages carefully, and reload the document. Avoid dog-eared or stapled pages in the ADF.
Scan quality is too low or too dark Adjust the Resolution (increase to 300 dpi) and Darkness settings on the scan settings screen.
