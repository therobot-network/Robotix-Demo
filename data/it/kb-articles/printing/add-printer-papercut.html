Add a Printer with PaperCut

Summary

Robotix IT uses PaperCut to manage all print queues across HP and Xerox multifunction printers (MFPs). This article walks you through adding a PaperCut-managed printer to your Windows 11 or macOS computer so you can print from any Robotix office location.

Before You Begin

  • You must be connected to the Robotix-Corp Wi-Fi network or the GlobalProtect VPN.
  • Sign in with your Robotix IT credentials (firstname.lastname@robotix.com) when prompted.
  • PaperCut is pre-installed on all Robotix-managed laptops. If you do not see it in your system tray (Windows) or menu bar (macOS), contact the Service Desk.
  • You need to know the name or location of the printer you want to add (e.g., Denver-Floor3-HP-MFP or Austin-Lobby-Xerox). Printer names are posted on labels affixed to each device.

Steps

Windows 11

  1. Click the PaperCut icon in your system tray (bottom-right corner of the taskbar). If it is not visible, click the up arrow to show hidden icons.
  2. Select Find a Printer from the PaperCut menu.
  3. In the search box, type the office location or printer name (e.g., Denver Floor 3). Matching printers appear in the list.
  4. Click the printer you want to add, then click Add Printer.
  5. Windows installs the print driver automatically. A confirmation notification appears when the printer is ready.
  6. To verify, open Settings > Bluetooth & devices > Printers & scanners and confirm the printer is listed.
  7. Print a test page by right-clicking the printer and choosing Printer properties > Print Test Page.

macOS

  1. Click the PaperCut icon in the menu bar (top-right).
  2. Select Find a Printer.
  3. Search by location or printer name. Click the printer you want and then click Add Printer.
  4. macOS may prompt you to enter your password to install the driver. Enter your Mac login password.
  5. Open System Settings > Printers & Scanners to confirm the new printer appears.
  6. Send a test print to verify the queue is working.

Troubleshooting

Symptom What to try
PaperCut icon is missing Open Company Portal (Windows) or contact the Service Desk — PaperCut may need to be reinstalled.
Printer does not appear in search results Confirm you are on Robotix-Corp Wi-Fi or connected via GlobalProtect VPN. Off-network printers are not discoverable.
"Access denied" error when adding printer Your Okta session may have expired. Lock and unlock your computer, or sign out of PaperCut and sign back in with your @robotix.com credentials.
Test page prints blank The toner or ink may be low. Check the printer's front panel display and report supply issues using KB-0604.
Driver installation fails on macOS Go to System Settings > Privacy & Security and allow the driver installer if prompted by Gatekeeper.
