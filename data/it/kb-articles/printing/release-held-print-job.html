Release a Held Print Job (Follow-Me / Secure Printing)

Summary

Robotix IT's PaperCut Follow-Me printing holds your documents in a secure queue until you authenticate at any compatible HP or Xerox MFP. Your print job is never left unattended in an output tray. This article explains how to release held jobs at the printer and through the PaperCut web portal.

Before You Begin

  • Follow-Me printing is enabled by default for all users. No setup is required on your computer.
  • Your Robotix Okta credentials (firstname.lastname@robotix.com) or your proximity badge (where configured) are used to authenticate at the printer.
  • Print jobs are held in the queue for 4 hours before they are automatically deleted to protect confidential information.
  • This feature works on all Robotix-managed HP and Xerox MFPs in every office location.

Steps

Release a Print Job at the Printer Panel

  1. Walk to any Robotix HP or Xerox MFP in your office.
  2. On the printer's touchscreen, tap PaperCut or Secure Print Release (the label may vary by model).
  3. Sign in using one of the following methods: - Badge tap: Hold your employee proximity badge near the card reader on the printer. Your jobs appear immediately. - Credentials: Type your Robotix username and password on the printer touchscreen, then tap Sign In.
  4. A list of your held print jobs appears on the screen.
  5. Tap the checkbox next to the job(s) you want to print, then tap Print (or Print All to release everything at once).
  6. Collect your pages from the output tray. The printer logs you out automatically after 60 seconds of inactivity.

Release or Delete a Job via the PaperCut Web Portal

  1. Open a browser and go to https://print.robotix.com (accessible on Robotix-Corp Wi-Fi or VPN).
  2. Sign in with your Okta credentials.
  3. Click Jobs Pending Release in the left navigation.
  4. Check the box next to the job(s) you want to act on.
  5. Click Release to send the job to a specific printer, or Delete to remove it from the queue.

Troubleshooting

Symptom What to try
My job does not appear on the printer screen Confirm you printed to the correct queue (e.g., Robotix-FollowMe or PaperCut-Secure). Printing to a direct printer queue bypasses Follow-Me.
Badge tap is not recognized Your badge may not yet be linked to PaperCut. Use your username/password to log in and then tap Link Badge on screen, or submit a request at https://help.robotix.com.
Job expired before I could release it Jobs are held for 4 hours. Reprint from your computer and release it promptly.
"No jobs available" message but I sent a print job Check whether the PaperCut client on your computer shows an error. Restarting the PaperCut Print Provider service (Windows) or reprinting may resolve it.
Printer prints someone else's job instead Each release session is isolated by credentials. If a mismatch occurred, log out immediately and notify the Service Desk so the event can be audited.
