Activate Microsoft 365 / Office on Your Device

Summary

All Robotix IT employees are licensed for Microsoft 365 Business Premium, which includes Outlook, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, OneNote, Teams, and OneDrive. Microsoft 365 apps are installed on your device during onboarding and activate automatically when you sign in with your Robotix IT account. This article walks you through the activation process and how to resolve common activation errors.

Before You Begin

  • Microsoft 365 must already be installed on your device. On Windows, check Company Portal; on Mac, check Self Service (see KB-0500). If Office is not installed, install it from the catalog before following these steps.
  • You need your Robotix IT credentials: firstname.lastname@robotix.com and your Okta password.
  • An active internet connection is required to complete activation. Connect to GlobalProtect VPN if you are off corporate network and experiencing activation issues.
  • Each Microsoft 365 license allows activation on up to 5 devices per user. If you have already activated on 5 devices, you will need to deactivate one via your Microsoft account before proceeding.

Steps

Windows

  1. Click the Start menu and open any Office app, such as Word or Outlook.
  2. If a sign-in prompt appears, click Sign In and enter your Robotix IT email address (firstname.lastname@robotix.com).
  3. You will be redirected to the Okta sign-in page. Enter your Okta password and approve the Okta Verify push notification on your mobile device.
  4. On the "Stay signed in?" prompt, click Yes to reduce the frequency of future sign-in prompts on this device.
  5. Office will activate in the background. A "License confirmed" or "You're all set" banner may briefly appear at the top of the app.
  6. To verify activation, open any Office app, click File > Account. Under Product Information, you should see Microsoft 365 Business Premium and your name.

macOS

  1. Open any Microsoft 365 app, such as Word or Outlook, from your Applications folder.
  2. If a "Sign In" or "Get Started" screen appears, click Sign In.
  3. Enter your Robotix IT email address (firstname.lastname@robotix.com) and click Next.
  4. On the Okta sign-in page, enter your password and approve the Okta Verify push notification.
  5. When activation succeeds, you will return to the app's main interface. A "Your license has been confirmed" message may appear briefly.
  6. To verify, open any Office app and go to the menu bar: click the App name (e.g., "Word") > About Word. The license should show Microsoft 365 subscription.

Sign In to OneDrive (Both Platforms)

  1. Open OneDrive from the Start menu (Windows) or Applications folder (Mac).
  2. Enter your Robotix IT email address and sign in via Okta.
  3. OneDrive will sync your files to C:\Users\<yourname>\OneDrive - Robotix Technologies (Windows) or ~/Library/CloudStorage/OneDrive-RobotixITServices (Mac).

Troubleshooting

"Account Notice: We found a problem with your Office subscription." This banner appears when activation cannot be verified. Ensure you are connected to the internet or VPN, then click Fix Now and sign in with your Robotix IT credentials.

"Sorry, we cannot connect to your account. Please try again later." This is usually a temporary Okta or Microsoft service issue. Wait 5 minutes and try again. Check https://status.office.com for any active Microsoft service incidents.

Office repeatedly asks you to sign in every time you open it. Open any Office app, go to File > Account (Windows) or Word > Account (Mac), click Sign Out, then sign back in. If the problem persists, open Company Portal or Self Service and reinstall Microsoft 365.

"Your organization has disabled the ability to use personal Microsoft accounts." You must sign in with your @robotix.com work account, not a personal Microsoft account. Close the dialog, click Use a work or school account instead, and re-enter your Robotix IT credentials.

Activation fails after signing in, showing "Product Deactivated." You may have reached the 5-device limit. Go to https://myaccount.microsoft.com, sign in with your Robotix IT account, navigate to Devices > Office Apps, and click Sign out next to a device you no longer use. Then retry activation.
