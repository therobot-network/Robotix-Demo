Install Approved Software Using Company Portal (Windows) or Self Service (Mac)

Summary

Robotix IT maintains a catalog of IT-approved applications that you can install yourself without opening a ticket. On Windows, this catalog is available through Company Portal. On Mac, it is available through Self Service. Both tools are pre-installed on your device and allow you to install, update, and reinstall approved software on demand.

Before You Begin

  • Your device must be enrolled in Intune (Windows) or Jamf Pro (Mac). All Robotix IT-issued laptops are enrolled automatically during setup.
  • You must be signed in to your device with your Robotix IT credentials (firstname.lastname@robotix.com).
  • An active internet connection is required. If you are off-site, connect to the GlobalProtect VPN before launching Company Portal or Self Service.
  • You do not need local administrator rights to install software from these catalogs.

Steps

Windows — Company Portal

  1. Click the Start menu and type Company Portal, then press Enter to open the app. If it is not pinned to your taskbar, you can also find it in the Microsoft Store.
  2. Sign in with your Robotix IT account (firstname.lastname@robotix.com) and complete the Okta Verify MFA prompt if asked.
  3. In the left navigation pane, click Apps.
  4. Browse the catalog or use the Search bar at the top to find the application you want.
  5. Click the application tile to open its detail page.
  6. Click Install. A progress bar will appear below the app name. Do not close Company Portal while the installation is in progress.
  7. Once the status changes to Installed, the application is ready to use. Find it in the Start menu or on your desktop.

macOS — Self Service

  1. Open Self Service from your Applications folder or the Dock. It is installed automatically on all Robotix IT Macs.
  2. Sign in with your Robotix IT credentials if prompted.
  3. Use the search bar in the top-right corner or browse the categories in the left sidebar to find the application you need.
  4. Click the application, then click Install. A progress indicator will appear on the button.
  5. When the button label changes to Reinstall or the progress indicator disappears, the installation is complete.
  6. Open the application from your Applications folder or Launchpad.

Troubleshooting

Company Portal shows "No apps available" or a blank catalog. Sign out of Company Portal, close the app, reopen it, and sign in again. If the catalog remains empty, confirm that your VPN is connected and contact the Service Desk.

Self Service fails with "Installation failed. Please try again later." Quit Self Service, wait two minutes, and try again. If the error persists, restart your Mac and retry. Software policies sync every 15 minutes from Jamf — a recent enrollment may require a brief wait.

The Install button in Company Portal is greyed out. The application may already be installed, or your device policy may require a restart before new installations are allowed. Check for a pending restart notification in the Windows taskbar.

The app does not appear in the catalog. Not all software is pre-approved for self-service install. If the application you need is not listed, submit a software request via the Service Desk portal (see KB-0501).

Installation appears stuck for more than 20 minutes. Cancel the install if the option is available, restart your device, and try again. If the issue continues, contact the Service Desk with the application name and the error message (if any).
