Robotix Employee Benefits Overview 2024

Effective January 1, 2024

At Robotix, we believe in providing comprehensive benefits that support our employees' well-being, financial security, and professional growth. This overview outlines the benefits available to full-time employees.

Health & Wellness Benefits

Medical Insurance

  • Three plan options: Basic PPO, Enhanced PPO, High-Deductible Health Plan with HSA
  • Company covers 80% of employee premium
  • Coverage begins first of the month following hire date
  • Nationwide provider network through Blue Cross Blue Shield

Dental Coverage

  • Delta Dental PPO plan
  • 100% coverage for preventive care
  • 80% coverage for basic services
  • 50% coverage for major services
  • $2,000 annual maximum benefit

Vision Insurance

  • 100% company-paid employee premium
  • Annual eye exam covered with $20 copay
  • $150 frame allowance every 12 months

Wellness Programs

  • On-site fitness center at headquarters
  • $50 monthly gym membership reimbursement
  • Quarterly wellness challenges with rewards
  • Annual biometric screenings

Financial Benefits

401(k) Retirement Plan

  • Immediate eligibility upon hire
  • 4% company match on first 5% contributed
  • Three-year graded vesting schedule
  • Wide range of investment options

Insurance Coverage

  • Life Insurance: 1x annual salary company-paid; optional supplemental up to 5x salary
  • Short-term Disability: 60% of salary up to 13 weeks
  • Long-term Disability: 66.67% of salary after 90 days

Time Off Benefits

Paid Time Off (PTO)

Years of Service Annual PTO Days
0-2 years 15 days
3-5 years 20 days
6+ years 25 days

Additional Time Off

  • 10 days annual sick leave
  • 8 paid holidays + 1 floating holiday
  • Parental Leave: 12 weeks primary caregiver, 4 weeks secondary
  • 16 hours annual volunteer time off

Professional Development

  • $5,000 annual tuition reimbursement
  • $1,000 annual conference/certification budget
  • Unlimited access to LinkedIn Learning
  • Internal mentorship program

Work-Life Benefits

  • Flexible Work: Hybrid schedule with up to 3 days remote
  • Employee Discount: 40% off Robotix products
  • Commuter Benefits:
    • Pre-tax transit benefits
    • $20 monthly bike-to-work benefit
    • Free parking
    • EV charging stations

Additional Perks

  • Fully stocked break rooms
  • Quarterly team building events
  • Annual summer picnic and holiday party
  • Service anniversary recognition

Enrollment & Questions

Benefits enrollment must be completed within 30 days of hire date. Annual open enrollment occurs each November for January 1 effective date.
