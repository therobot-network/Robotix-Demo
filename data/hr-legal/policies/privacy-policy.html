Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

At Robotix ("we," "us," or "our"), we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our websites, mobile applications, robotic products, and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Contact information (name, email, phone, address)
  • Payment details (credit card tokens, billing address)
  • Purchase history and product preferences
  • Account credentials and settings
  • Customer service communications

1.2 Automated Collection

  • IP address and device identifiers
  • Browser type and settings
  • Geographic location (if enabled)
  • Operating system information
  • Robot telemetry data including:
    • Usage patterns and operational statistics
    • Performance metrics and diagnostic data
    • Environmental sensor readings
    • Software version and update status

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Services

  • Processing and fulfilling orders
  • Providing customer support
  • Managing your account
  • Enabling robot functionality and updates
  • Analyzing product performance and safety

2.2 Business Operations

  • Improving products and services
  • Detecting and preventing fraud
  • Conducting market research
  • Maintaining security measures

3. Information Sharing

We share information with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, shipping carriers, cloud storage providers
  • Analytics Partners: Google Analytics, Mixpanel
  • Legal Requirements: Law enforcement, regulatory agencies, court orders

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • AES-256 encryption for stored data
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Multi-factor authentication for system access
  • Employee security training and access controls

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Export your data in a portable format

6. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA. We do not sell personal information but collect and share as described in this policy.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period
Account Information 7 years after closure
Robot Telemetry 2 years
Payment Records 7 years

9. Updates to Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be notified via email or website notice. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance.
