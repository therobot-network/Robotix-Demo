Remote Work Policy

Effective Date: March 15, 2024
Last Updated: March 1, 2024
Document Owner: Human Resources

1. Purpose and Scope

This policy establishes guidelines for remote work arrangements at Robotix, enabling eligible employees to work from locations outside our facilities while maintaining productivity, collaboration, and security standards. This policy applies to all Robotix employees except those in manufacturing, hardware testing, and warehouse operations.

2. Eligibility Requirements

  • Completion of 90-day probationary period
  • Satisfactory performance record
  • Role compatibility with remote work
  • Direct supervisor and department head approval
  • Demonstrated ability to work independently
  • Home office setup meeting company requirements

3. Remote Work Options

Hybrid Schedule: Up to 3 days per week remote work permitted
Full Remote: Available on case-by-case basis with executive approval

Department Maximum Remote Days Special Requirements
Software Development 3 days/week Team sprint days in-office
Manufacturing 0 days/week On-site presence required
Sales/Marketing 3 days/week Client meetings as needed

4. Technology Requirements

  • Minimum internet speed: 25 Mbps download/5 Mbps upload
  • Dedicated workspace with appropriate lighting for video calls
  • Company-provided laptop with current security software
  • Mandatory VPN usage for company network access
  • Dual-factor authentication for all system access
  • Backup power solution recommended

5. Equipment and Expenses

  • One-time home office setup stipend: $500
  • Monthly internet reimbursement: $50
  • Company-provided equipment:
    • Laptop computer
    • Monitor
    • Keyboard and mouse
    • Headset for video conferences

6. Work Hours and Availability

  • Core hours: 10:00 AM - 3:00 PM Pacific Time
  • Maximum response time: 2 hours during work hours
  • Status must be current in Microsoft Teams
  • Calendar must be kept up to date
  • Overtime requires advance supervisor approval

7. Communication Standards

  • Cameras on for all video meetings
  • Weekly one-on-one check-ins with supervisor
  • Daily team standups attendance required
  • Use of designated collaboration tools (Teams, Slack, JIRA)

8. Required In-Office Events

  • Quarterly all-hands meetings
  • Team building events
  • Critical project kickoffs
  • Performance reviews
  • Training sessions marked as "in-person required"

9. Security and Confidentiality

Remote workers must maintain the same level of security and confidentiality as in-office workers, including:

  • Secure workspace free from unauthorized viewing
  • Proper document disposal
  • No public WiFi usage
  • Regular security training completion

10. Policy Violations

Violations may result in:

  • Verbal warning
  • Written warning
  • Remote work privilege suspension
  • Termination of remote work agreement

11. Application Process

To request remote work arrangement:

  1. Complete Remote Work Application Form
  2. Obtain supervisor approval
  3. Submit home office photos and internet speed test results
  4. Complete required security training
  5. Sign Remote Work Agreement

This policy is subject to change based on business needs and performance metrics. Robotix reserves the right to modify or terminate remote work arrangements at any time.
