Product Overview

The CoBot Mobile Platform is an advanced collaborative mobile robot designed for flexible manufacturing environments, warehouses, and logistics operations. This platform combines autonomous navigation with collaborative capabilities, enabling seamless human-robot interaction while maintaining the highest safety standards.

Target Applications

  • Material handling and transportation
  • Just-in-time parts delivery
  • Warehouse order fulfillment
  • Production line supply
  • Cross-docking operations

Technical Specifications

Physical Characteristics

Dimensions (L×W×H) 1200 × 700 × 450 mm
Weight 185 kg (unloaded)
Payload Capacity 500 kg
Ground Clearance 35 mm

Drive System

  • Dual brushless DC motors (48V, 750W each)
  • Mecanum wheel configuration for omnidirectional movement
  • Maximum speed: 2.0 m/s
  • Acceleration: 0.5 m/s²
  • Turning radius: 0° (on-spot rotation)

Control System

  • Industrial PC with Intel Core i7 processor
  • ROS 2 compatible
  • Real-time control loop: 1000 Hz
  • Programming interfaces: REST API, Python SDK
  • Wireless connectivity: Wi-Fi 6E, Bluetooth 5.2

Sensor Suite

  • 2× 3D LiDAR sensors (270° FOV each)
  • 6× Time-of-Flight sensors for close-range detection
  • 4× RGB-D cameras for obstacle detection and navigation
  • IMU for position tracking
  • Wheel encoders for odometry

Power System

  • Battery: 48V, 40Ah Lithium Iron Phosphate
  • Operating time: 12 hours (typical use)
  • Charging time: 3 hours (0-100%)
  • Auto-docking capability

Performance Metrics

  • Positioning accuracy: ±10 mm
  • Angular accuracy: ±1°
  • Maximum slope capability: 8%
  • Minimum obstacle height detection: 5 mm
  • Operating temperature: 5°C to 40°C
  • IP54 protection rating

Safety Features

  • ISO/TS 15066 compliant
  • 360° safety field monitoring
  • Emergency stop buttons (2×)
  • Dynamic speed adjustment based on human proximity
  • Fail-safe braking system
  • Visual and audible warning indicators

Manufacturing & Quality

Manufactured at Robotix Minneapolis Facility under ISO 9001:2015 certification. Each unit undergoes comprehensive testing including:

  • 48-hour continuous operation test
  • Full payload capacity verification
  • Safety system validation
  • Environmental chamber testing

Pricing & Warranty

  • MSRP: $27,500
  • Warranty: 2-year limited warranty on all components
  • Extended warranty: Available up to 5 years
  • Technical support: 24/7 phone and email support

Packaging & Shipping

  • Shipping dimensions: 1400 × 900 × 600 mm
  • Shipping weight: 225 kg
  • Includes: Platform, charging station, documentation, basic tool kit
  • Optional accessories available separately
