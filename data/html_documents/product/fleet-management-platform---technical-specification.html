Product Overview

The Robotix Fleet Management Platform (FMP) is an enterprise-grade software solution designed to orchestrate, monitor, and optimize industrial robot fleets across manufacturing facilities. This platform enables centralized control, real-time monitoring, and predictive maintenance capabilities for diverse robotic systems.

Key Features

  • Real-time fleet monitoring and control
  • Predictive maintenance analytics
  • Multi-site deployment capability
  • Integration with major industrial protocols
  • Advanced security and access control
