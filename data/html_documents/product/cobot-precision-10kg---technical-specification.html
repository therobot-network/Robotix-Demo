CoBot Precision 10kg Technical Specification

Product Overview

The CoBot Precision 10kg is an advanced collaborative robot designed for high-precision manufacturing operations requiring consistent accuracy and human-robot collaboration. This next-generation cobot delivers industry-leading repeatability while maintaining the highest safety standards for human-robot interaction.

Target Applications

  • Precision assembly
  • Machine tending
  • Quality inspection
  • Pick-and-place operations
  • Laboratory automation
  • Electronics manufacturing
