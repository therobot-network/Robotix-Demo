Technical Specification: AutoNav AGV-500

Product Overview

The AutoNav AGV-500 is an advanced autonomous guided vehicle designed for intelligent material handling and logistics operations in manufacturing, warehousing, and distribution facilities. This next-generation mobile robot leverages AI-powered navigation and dynamic obstacle avoidance to enable flexible, efficient automation of material transport.

Key Innovations

  • Proprietary SimulNav™ simultaneous localization and mapping (SLAM) technology
  • Multi-modal navigation combining LiDAR, cameras, and inertial sensors
  • Dynamic fleet optimization with RobotixFleet™ management system
  • Tool-free battery swap system with hot-swapping capability

Technical Specifications

Chassis & Construction

  • Frame: Aircraft-grade aluminum alloy
  • Dimensions (L x W x H): 1200 x 800 x 450 mm
  • Ground Clearance: 50 mm
  • Vehicle Weight: 185 kg
  • Maximum Payload: 500 kg

Drive System

  • Drive Type: Differential drive with dual AC servo motors
  • Motor Power: 2 x 750W brushless DC
  • Wheel Configuration: 2 drive wheels, 4 support casters
  • Turning Radius: Zero (point turn capable)

Control & Navigation

  • Controller: RBX-800 industrial controller with ARM Cortex-A72
  • Operating System: RobotixOS 4.5
  • Navigation Sensors:
    • 360° LiDAR scanner (30m range)
    • 8 ultrasonic sensors
    • 4 3D depth cameras
    • IMU with 9-axis sensing
  • Connectivity: WiFi 6, Ethernet, 4G LTE (optional)

Performance Specifications

Maximum Speed 2.0 m/s
Positioning Accuracy ±10 mm
Runtime (full charge) 12 hours
Charging Time 1.5 hours (0-80%)

Power System

  • Battery: 48V, 100Ah Li-ion
  • Charging: 220V AC, automated docking
  • Power Consumption: 400W (typical)

Environmental Specifications

  • Operating Temperature: 5°C to 40°C
  • Humidity: 10-90% non-condensing
  • IP Rating: IP54
  • Floor Condition: Smooth concrete, epoxy or similar
  • Maximum Slope: 3%

Safety Features

  • 360° safety scanning with dynamic safety zones
  • Emergency stop buttons (2 sides)
  • Warning lights and audio indicators
  • Bump sensors with instant stop
  • Safety certifications: CE, UL 1740
