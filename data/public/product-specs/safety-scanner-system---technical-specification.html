Product Overview

The RBX-SSS-2024 Safety Scanner System is Robotix's next-generation safety monitoring solution designed for industrial and collaborative robot applications. This advanced laser scanning system provides 275° coverage with configurable safety zones, enabling dynamic workspace monitoring and personnel protection.

Target Applications

  • Collaborative robot workspaces
  • Automated manufacturing cells
  • Mobile robot navigation
  • Automated guided vehicle (AGV) systems
  • Hazardous area monitoring
