Installation Instructions

Step 1: Initial Setup

Unpack unit in a clean, level area with minimum 3m × 3m clear space. Verify all components against packing list. Check for any shipping damage.

Step 2: Base Installation

Position base unit on level surface. Adjust leveling feet to achieve ±0.5° levelness. Torque mounting bolts to 45 Nm.

Step 3: Electrical Connections

Connect power supply (48V DC), verify polarity. Install emergency stop circuit using provided 4mm² cable. Connect safety scanner inputs to designated terminals.
