Product Overview

The Welding Robot Pro (WRP-3000) is Robotix's flagship industrial welding robot, designed for high-precision automated welding applications in heavy manufacturing environments. This 6-axis articulated robot delivers exceptional accuracy, repeatability, and durability for demanding production schedules.

Target Applications

  • Automated MIG/MAG welding
  • TIG welding operations
  • Spot welding
  • Heavy equipment manufacturing
  • Automotive assembly lines
  • Shipbuilding operations
