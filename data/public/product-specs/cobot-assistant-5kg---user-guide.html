Installation Instructions

Step 1: Unpacking and Inspection

Remove all components carefully. Verify against packing list. Check for shipping damage. Report any discrepancies to 1-800-555-ROBO within 24 hours.

Step 2: Mounting Installation

Secure mounting plate to stable surface rated for minimum 100kg load. Use M8 grade 8.8 bolts torqued to 24Nm. Ensure surface is level within 0.1°.

Step 3: Electrical Connections

Connect power supply, emergency stop circuit, and safety sensors according to provided wiring diagram. Verify ground connection resistance below 100mΩ.
