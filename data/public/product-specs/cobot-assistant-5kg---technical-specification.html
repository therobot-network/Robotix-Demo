Product Overview

The CoBot Assistant 5kg is a next-generation collaborative robot designed for precision handling and assembly operations in manufacturing environments. This versatile cobot delivers enhanced safety features, intuitive programming, and seamless integration capabilities for Industry 4.0 applications.

Target Applications

  • Light assembly and material handling
  • Pick-and-place operations
  • Quality inspection and testing
  • Machine tending
  • Packaging and palletizing

Key Innovations

  • Advanced force sensing with 0.1N resolution
  • AI-powered collision detection
  • Integrated vision system with deep learning capabilities
  • Quick-change tool interface with auto-recognition
