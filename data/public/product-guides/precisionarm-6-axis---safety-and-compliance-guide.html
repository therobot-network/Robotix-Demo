5. Emergency Procedures

In case of emergency, follow these procedures:

Emergency Stop

  1. Press any emergency stop button immediately
  2. Ensure the robot has come to a complete stop
  3. Do not reset until the cause is identified and resolved
  4. Report the incident to your supervisor
  5. Document the event in the safety log

Emergency Contacts

  • On-site Emergency: Call 911
  • Safety Manager: Extension 5555
  • Technical Support: 1-800-ROBOTIX (24/7)
  • Supervisor: Per facility directory
