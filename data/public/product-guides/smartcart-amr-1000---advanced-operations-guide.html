1. Advanced Motion Control

Advanced motion control features for optimized performance:

Trajectory Blending

Use zone parameters to blend motions for smoother paths:

  • z1 - 1mm radius blend (tight corners)
  • z10 - 10mm radius blend (balanced)
  • z50 - 50mm radius blend (high speed)
  • fine - No blending (precise stop)
ðŸ’¡ Tip: Using zone parameters instead of fine positioning can reduce cycle time by 20-40%.

3. Force Control Applications

Implement force-controlled operations for delicate tasks:

Contact Detection

ForceMode(TCP, [0,0,1,0,0,0], [0,0,20,0,0,0], 2, [.1,.1,.1,.1,.1,.1])

Parameters:

  • Reference frame: TCP
  • Selection vector: Z-axis only
  • Force limits: 20N in Z-direction
  • Type: 2 (compliant mode)
  • Limits: Motion limits per axis

Applications

  • Part insertion and assembly
  • Surface following and deburring
  • Quality inspection with probing
  • Delicate material handling
