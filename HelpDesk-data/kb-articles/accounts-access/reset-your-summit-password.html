Reset Your Summit Password (Okta Self-Service Reset)

Summary

If you have forgotten your Summit password or want to change it proactively, you can reset it yourself through the Okta self-service reset flow — no help desk ticket required. The reset takes about two minutes and your new password is effective immediately across all Summit apps.

Before You Begin

  • You must have previously set up at least one recovery factor in Okta (Okta Verify, a recovery email, or a security question). If you have never logged in before or skipped recovery setup, contact the Service Desk instead.
  • Have your mobile device nearby if Okta Verify push notifications are your primary recovery method.
  • Your new password must meet Summit's complexity policy: at least 12 characters, at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one special character. Passwords cannot match any of your last 10 passwords.

Steps

  1. Open a browser and navigate to https://summitit.okta.com.
  2. On the sign-in page, click Need help signing in? below the password field, then click Forgot password.
  3. Enter your Summit email address (firstname.lastname@summitit.com) and click Next.
  4. Okta will prompt you to verify your identity. Choose a recovery method: - Okta Verify push — tap Approve on the push notification on your phone. - Email code — check your personal recovery email address for a 6-digit code and enter it within 10 minutes. - Security question — answer the question you configured during enrollment.
  5. After successful verification, you will land on the Reset Password screen.
  6. Type your new password in the New password field, then re-enter it in the Confirm new password field.
  7. Click Reset Password. Okta will validate complexity requirements in real time — red indicators turn green as each requirement is met.
  8. When you see Your password has been reset, click Back to sign in and log in with your new password.
  9. If you are on a Windows 11 device joined to the domain, lock and unlock your PC (Win + L, then sign in) or restart to sync the new password to your local Windows credential cache.

Troubleshooting

Symptom Likely Cause Fix
"We can't find an account with that email" Typo in email address Re-enter your full @summitit.com address
Push notification never arrives Phone is offline or Okta Verify app needs update Switch to the email code method; update the app when back online
New password rejected Doesn't meet complexity requirements or is a recently used password Choose a password not used in the past 10 resets and ensure all character classes are present
Password reset but Windows still prompts for old password Local credential cache not synced Lock and unlock the PC, or restart; if on VPN this sync is automatic
Recovery email code expired Codes are valid for 10 minutes only Click Resend code on the Okta screen to get a new one
