Request a New User Account (for Managers)

Summary

Before a new employee starts, their manager must submit a New User Account request through the Summit Service Desk portal. IT uses this request to provision the Okta account, Microsoft 365 license, laptop, and any application access on day one. Requests submitted fewer than three business days before the start date may result in delayed access.

Before You Begin

  • You must be the new hire's direct manager or an authorized delegate.
  • You will need the following information — collect it before starting the form to avoid saving a partial submission:
  • Legal first and last name (as it appears on government ID)
  • Personal email address (for the welcome email and Okta enrollment link)
  • Personal mobile phone number (for MFA enrollment)
  • Start date
  • Job title and department
  • Office location or "Remote" with city and state
  • Employment type: Full-time, Part-time, Contractor, or Intern
  • Cost center / billing code
  • Any non-standard application access required (standard access is provisioned based on job title)
  • Hardware preference if your department has a choice (Windows laptop or Mac)
  • Requests for contractor accounts require a completed Vendor Access Agreement on file with Legal before IT will provision access. Contact your Legal liaison if unsure.

Steps

  1. Log in to the Summit Service Desk portal at https://help.summitit.com.
  2. Under the Service Catalog, click Accounts & Access, then select New User Account Request.
  3. Complete the Employee Details section: - Legal Name: First and last name exactly as on government ID. - Preferred Name: (Optional) Name to display in Teams and Outlook. - Personal Email: Used to send the Okta activation email to the new hire. - Personal Mobile: For MFA setup. Must be a number the employee can access before their first day. - Start Date: Select from the calendar picker. Minimum 3 business days from today.
  4. Complete the Role Details section: - Job Title, Department, Cost Center. - Manager: Your name should pre-populate; confirm it is correct. - Office Location or Remote City/State. - Employment Type: Select Full-time, Part-time, Contractor, or Intern.
  5. Complete the Access Requirements section: - The form auto-selects a standard access template based on the job title you entered. Review the list of applications that will be provisioned. - To add a non-standard application, click + Add Application and search by name. - To remove an application from the standard set, uncheck it and provide a brief justification.
  6. In the Hardware section, select the laptop platform (Windows — Dell Latitude 7440 or Mac — MacBook Pro 14"). If no preference, leave as Default (Windows).
  7. In the Additional Notes field, add any special instructions (e.g., "Employee works overnight shift," "Requires Spanish-language OS," "Ship hardware to home address: [address]").
  8. Click Submit Request. You receive a confirmation email with a ticket number in the format REQ-######.
  9. IT will email you when provisioning is complete (typically 1–2 business days after submission for standard requests). The new hire receives their Okta activation email to their personal address — remind them to check spam.

Okta account Username firstname.lastname@summitit.com
Microsoft 365 License assigned; Outlook, Teams, OneDrive, SharePoint

Laptop Configured and shipped or available for pickup based on location

Troubleshooting

Symptom Likely Cause Fix
Start date is grayed out for dates fewer than 3 business days away Rush requests require manager VP approval Call the Service Desk to discuss a rush provisioning — additional SLA applies
Non-standard app not appearing in the search App is not integrated with Okta yet Note it in Additional Notes and IT will handle it manually or escalate to L3
New hire did not receive activation email Personal email address was entered incorrectly Reply to your confirmation ticket with the correct address; IT will resend
Access template looks wrong for the role Job title not yet in the provisioning system Select the closest matching title and add a note describing the correct access
