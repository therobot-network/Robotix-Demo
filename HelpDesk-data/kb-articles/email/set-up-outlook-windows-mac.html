Set Up Outlook on Windows and Mac

Summary

This article walks you through installing and configuring Microsoft Outlook on your Summit IT-managed Windows 11 or macOS device. Outlook is part of the Microsoft 365 suite and is the standard email client at Summit IT Services. Your account uses Okta Single Sign-On (SSO), so you will authenticate through Okta during setup.

Before You Begin

  • Your device must be enrolled in Intune (Windows) or Jamf Pro (macOS).
  • You must have your Summit IT credentials: firstname.lastname@summitit.com and your Okta password.
  • Okta Verify must be installed and enrolled on your phone for multi-factor authentication (MFA).
  • If you are off-network, connect to GlobalProtect VPN before proceeding.

Steps

Windows 11 — Install and Configure Outlook

  1. Open Company Portal from the Start menu or taskbar.
  2. Sign in with your firstname.lastname@summitit.com account if prompted.
  3. Go to Apps and locate Microsoft 365 Apps. Click Install. The installation may take 5–15 minutes depending on your connection speed.
  4. Once installation completes, open Outlook from the Start menu.
  5. On the "Enter your email address" screen, type your full Summit IT email address (firstname.lastname@summitit.com) and click Connect.
  6. Outlook will redirect you to the Okta login page. Enter your Okta password and approve the Okta Verify push notification on your phone.
  7. On the "Account successfully added" screen, click Done. Uncheck "Set up Outlook Mobile on my phone too" if you don't need it right now.
  8. Outlook will begin syncing your mailbox. Initial sync of a large mailbox can take 10–30 minutes.

macOS — Install and Configure Outlook

  1. Open Self Service from your Applications folder or Dock (installed by Jamf Pro).
  2. Search for Microsoft 365 and click Install.
  3. Once the installation completes, open Outlook from your Applications folder.
  4. Click Get Started on the welcome screen.
  5. Enter your Summit IT email address (firstname.lastname@summitit.com) and click Continue.
  6. You will be taken to the Okta login page in a browser window. Enter your credentials and approve the MFA push from Okta Verify.
  7. Grant Outlook any requested permissions (Keychain access, notifications).
  8. Click Done. Your mailbox will begin syncing.

Verify Your Setup

  • Confirm your inbox displays your Summit IT email.
  • Send a test message to a colleague.
  • Check that your calendar entries appear under the Calendar tab.

Troubleshooting

Symptom Action
Okta login page does not appear Ensure you are connected to the internet or GlobalProtect VPN; restart Outlook and try again.
"Account already exists" error Open File > Account Settings (Windows) or Outlook > Preferences > Accounts (macOS) and remove any duplicate entries before re-adding.
Outlook crashes at launch on macOS Open Self Service and run the Repair Microsoft Office script, or restart the device.
Mailbox not syncing Go to File > Account Settings > Account Settings, select your account, click Change, and verify the server settings resolve to outlook.office365.com.
MFA push not arriving Open Okta Verify, ensure notifications are allowed, and tap Refresh on the pending request.
