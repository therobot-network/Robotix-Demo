Enroll a Device in Intune with the Company Portal App

Summary

Microsoft Intune is Summit IT's mobile device management (MDM) platform. Enrolling your device registers it with Summit IT so you can access corporate resources like email, Teams, and SharePoint from your phone or tablet. This article covers enrollment for both company-issued devices and personal BYOD devices.

Before You Begin

  • You need your Summit IT credentials: firstname.lastname@summitit.com and your password.
  • Okta Verify must be installed and configured on your device before enrollment. See KB-0608 if you need to set up Okta Verify.
  • For company-issued devices, IT may have pre-enrolled the device. Check whether Microsoft Intune already appears under Settings before starting.
  • For personal (BYOD) devices, enrollment uses a work profile that keeps personal and work data separate. See KB-0610 to understand what IT can and cannot see.
  • Enrollment requires a stable Wi-Fi connection. Using cellular is possible but not recommended for the initial enrollment.

Steps

iOS (iPhone / iPad)

  1. Open the App Store and search for Company Portal (publisher: Microsoft Corporation). Install the app.
  2. Open Company Portal and tap Sign in.
  3. Enter your Summit email address (firstname.lastname@summitit.com) and tap Next.
  4. You are redirected to the Okta sign-in page. Enter your Summit password and complete the Okta Verify push notification on your device.
  5. Tap Begin on the "Set up access" screen, then tap Continue through the information screens.
  6. When prompted, tap Allow to install the Summit MDM configuration profile.
  7. Open the Settings app and navigate to General > VPN & Device Management. Tap the Summit IT Services profile and tap Install. Enter your device passcode if prompted, then tap Install again to confirm.
  8. Return to Company Portal and tap Continue. The app verifies compliance (this takes up to 2 minutes).
  9. Enrollment is complete when you see the "You're all set!" screen. Tap Done.

Android

  1. Open the Google Play Store, search for Intune Company Portal (publisher: Microsoft Corporation), and install it.
  2. Open Company Portal and tap Sign in.
  3. Enter your Summit email address and tap Next. Complete Okta authentication including the push notification.
  4. Tap Begin and follow the on-screen prompts.
  5. When asked to set up a Work Profile, tap Next and then Accept to allow Company Portal to create the work profile. This installs a separate work container — your personal apps and data remain untouched.
  6. Tap Continue to activate the work profile. Your device may ask for your PIN or biometric.
  7. The Company Portal app moves into the work profile. Re-open it from the work profile section of your app drawer (indicated by a briefcase icon).
  8. Sign in again within the work profile when prompted. Compliance verification runs automatically.
  9. Tap Done when enrollment completes. Work apps will appear in your work profile.

Troubleshooting

Symptom What to try
Sign-in fails with "Not enrolled in Okta" Set up Okta Verify first (KB-0608), then retry enrollment.
iOS profile fails to install Make sure you go to Settings > General > VPN & Device Management immediately after tapping Allow — the profile download times out after a few minutes.
Android work profile setup fails Ensure your Android version is 8.0 or later. Go to Settings > Apps > Company Portal > Uninstall, reinstall from the Play Store, and retry.
Compliance check runs indefinitely Restart Company Portal. If the issue persists after 10 minutes, restart your device.
"Device limit reached" error Summit allows up to 5 enrolled devices per user. Submit a request at https://help.summitit.com to remove an old device.
